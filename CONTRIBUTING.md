# Contributing to Awesome AI PM

Thanks for helping make this resource better. Here is how to suggest a new resource.

## What Makes a Good Resource

- Directly relevant to AI product management
- High quality: well-written, accurate, actionable
- Publicly accessible (no paywalled content without a free tier)
- Not purely promotional or self-serving

## How to Submit a PR

1. Fork this repository
2. Add your resource to the appropriate section in README.md
3. Use the existing link format: `[Title](URL) — One-line description`
4. If no section fits, propose a new one with at least 3 resources
5. Open a pull request with:
   - A clear title (e.g., "Add resource: AI Pricing Guide by X")
   - A short explanation of why this resource is valuable

## Review Process

- PRs are reviewed weekly
- Maintainers may suggest edits or a different section
- Duplicate or low-quality submissions will be closed with an explanation
- Once approved, your contribution will be merged and credited

## Questions?

Open an issue if you are unsure whether a resource fits.
